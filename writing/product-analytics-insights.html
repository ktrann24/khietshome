Product Analytics: From Data to Insights

December 8, 2024

During my time at Amplitude, I learned that good product analytics isn't just about collecting data—it's about transforming that data into insights that drive meaningful product decisions.

The Data Problem

Most companies are drowning in data but starving for insights. The challenge isn't collecting more data; it's knowing what to measure, how to interpret it, and how to turn those interpretations into action.

Framework for Success

A successful analytics practice requires:

  • Clear definitions of what success looks like
  • Instrumentation that captures user intent, not just actions
  • Tools that make exploration and discovery easy
  • A culture that values data-informed decision making

Making It Actionable

The best insights are useless if they don't lead to action. Build tight feedback loops between analytics, product development, and user research. Make your data accessible to everyone who needs it, not just data scientists.
